01 / 08

Overview

A direct account of what Blunt38 handles—and what it does not.

This policy applies to the Blunt38 Discord bot, its web dashboard, and its Draw Party browser game. It does not cover Discord itself or third-party services you access independently.

Blunt38 processes only the information needed to provide features selected by server administrators or requested by users. Eclipxse does not sell personal data or use it to build advertising profiles.

02 / 08

Data processed

The exact data depends on the features enabled in a Discord server.

Nothing here is collected to create an advertising profile.

Discord identifiers

User, server, channel, role, message, moderator, and temporary voice-channel IDs.

Server settings

Configured channels and roles, welcome text, logs, tickets, AI settings, and appearance preferences.

Feature records

Moderation cases, polls and votes, role panels, giveaways, XP and levels, birthday month/day, and temporary voice ownership.

Submitted content

Command inputs, suggestions, ticket messages used for requested transcripts, and messages sent to enabled AI features.

Dashboard account

Your Discord ID, username, avatar URL, and the IDs of servers you are permitted to manage.

Draw Party

Player name, socket ID, room membership, guesses, drawing strokes, scores, and sound preference.

Important Blunt38 never requests your Discord password. Authentication is handled by Discord OAuth.

03 / 08

How it is used

Data has a job. If a feature does not need it, Blunt38 does not need it.

  • 01Respond to commands and Discord interactions.
  • 02Run moderation, tickets, roles, polls, giveaways, levels, birthdays, welcome messages, and temporary voice channels.
  • 03Authenticate server administrators and save dashboard configuration.
  • 04Provide optional AI replies, music playback, and Draw Party rooms.
  • 05Prevent abuse, diagnose failures, and protect the service.
  • 06Meet lawful obligations and enforce applicable platform rules.

No data sales.

No ad profiles.

04 / 08

AI + providers

Optional features sometimes need another service to finish the job.

When an AI feature is used, relevant message content, instructions, and limited context may be sent to the AI provider configured by the operator: OpenAI, Groq, or OpenRouter.

Blunt38 does not store AI prompts in its feature database, although Discord and the selected provider may retain data under their own policies.

Discord

Interactions, OAuth, messages, servers, roles, channels, and voice.

Database hosting

Supabase or the configured PostgreSQL host for persistent settings and records.

AI providers

OpenAI, Groq, or OpenRouter when an optional AI feature is requested.

Music services

Lavalink and supported sources for searches, links, and playback.

Web infrastructure

Hosting, CDN, security, diagnostics, and availability.

Eclipxse does not allow these providers to use bot data for Eclipxse advertising.

05 / 08

Storage + retention

OAuth state

Approximately 10 minutes.

Dashboard session

Up to 7 days; stored in a signed, HTTP-only cookie.

Draw Party room

In memory while active; inactive rooms expire after approximately 3 hours.

Server settings and feature records

Until deleted, no longer required, or removal is requested and verified.

Ticket transcript

Generated on request from recent Discord messages and delivered through Discord.

Your browser may remember your Draw Party name and sound preference.

Removing Blunt38 from a server does not automatically erase all database records. A server owner may request deletion.

06 / 08

Your choices

Access it. Correct it. Delete it.

Server administrators can disable optional features, change configuration, clear browser storage, or remove Blunt38. You may request access, correction, or deletion of data associated with your Discord account or server.

Include your Discord user ID and any relevant server ID. Additional verification may be required to prevent unauthorized disclosure or deletion. Verified requests are normally handled within 30 days.

07 / 08

Safety + age

Protection is practical, not a promise of the impossible.

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect data, including restricted credentials, signed sessions, and access checks. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Blunt38 is intended for people permitted to use Discord under Discord's terms. It is not directed to children below the minimum age required in their country. If you believe a child provided data improperly, contact Eclipxse for deletion.

This policy may change when Blunt38's features or providers change. Material revisions will update the date at the top of this page.
