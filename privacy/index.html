01

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Blunt38, including the Discord bot, its optional web dashboard, and its browser-based Draw Party game. Blunt38 is operated by Eclipxse.

Discord separately processes information under its own privacy policy. Server owners and administrators also control how Blunt38 is configured in their Discord servers and may have their own rules for server content.

Blunt38 processes data only to provide requested bot features, administer connected servers, maintain security, and respond to support or privacy requests. It does not sell Discord data or use it for targeted advertising.
02

Data Blunt38 processes

The exact data depends on which features a server administrator enables and which commands users choose to run.

Discord identifiers

Server, user, moderator, channel, message, role, and temporary voice-channel identifiers.

Server configuration

Selected channels and roles, welcome text, log settings, ticket settings, AI configuration, and visual preferences.

Feature records

Moderation actions and reasons, polls and votes, role panels, giveaway entries, XP and levels, birthday month/day, and temporary voice ownership.

Command and message content

Command inputs, suggestions, ticket content processed for on-demand transcripts, and messages submitted to enabled AI features.

Dashboard account data

Discord user ID, username, avatar URL, and the IDs of servers you are allowed to manage.

Draw Party data

Player name, socket identifier, room membership, guesses, drawing strokes, scores, and sound preference.

The bot may also receive technical logs needed to diagnose errors or abuse. Hosting and network providers may process IP addresses, request paths, browser or device details, and timestamps when you use the dashboard, game, or this policy page.

03

How data is used

  • Respond to commands and interactions.
  • Operate moderation, tickets, roles, polls, giveaways, levels, birthdays, welcome messages, and temporary voice channels.
  • Authenticate authorized server administrators and save dashboard configuration.
  • Provide optional AI replies, music playback, and browser game rooms.
  • Prevent abuse, troubleshoot failures, and protect the bot and connected servers.
  • Comply with lawful requests and enforce applicable platform rules.

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, processing is based on providing the feature you or a server administrator requested, legitimate interests in operating and securing the service, consent where required for optional features, and compliance with legal obligations.

04

AI features

If a server administrator enables AI replies, Blunt38 may send the relevant message content together with the server name, channel name, username, and configured bot prompt to the AI provider selected by the operator. The supported providers are OpenAI, Groq, and OpenRouter; only the configured provider receives a given request.

Blunt38 does not store AI prompt content in its feature database. The original message and the bot's reply may remain in Discord, and the selected AI provider may retain or process requests under its own terms and privacy policy. Do not submit passwords, financial data, health information, or other sensitive personal data to AI commands.

05

Dashboard, cookies, and local storage

The optional dashboard uses Discord OAuth with the identify and guilds scopes. It creates a signed, HTTP-only login cookie containing your Discord identity, manageable server IDs, and expiry time. The login cookie lasts up to seven days. A separate OAuth security-state cookie lasts up to ten minutes.

Discord access tokens are used during sign-in to request your identity and server list; they are not included in Blunt38's dashboard session cookie. The Draw Party game stores the player name and sound preference in the user's browser using local storage so they can be reused on the same device.

06

Service providers and sharing

Data is shared only when needed to operate a requested feature, comply with law, or protect the service. Relevant providers may include:

  • Discord — bot interactions, OAuth, messages, servers, roles, channels, voice, and platform delivery.
  • Supabase or the operator's PostgreSQL host — persistent feature and configuration storage when database mode is enabled.
  • OpenAI, Groq, or OpenRouter — optional AI requests, depending on the configured provider.
  • Lavalink and music sources — music search terms, links, and playback requests.
  • Hosting, CDN, and infrastructure providers — delivery, network security, diagnostics, and uptime.

Blunt38 does not sell, rent, or disclose Discord API data to data brokers, advertising networks, or marketing services. Information may be disclosed if required by law, to investigate abuse, or to protect users, Discord, Blunt38, or others.

07

Retention

Dashboard loginUp to 7 days
OAuth security stateUp to 10 minutes
Draw Party server roomsUp to 3 hours
Draw Party browser preferencesUntil cleared by the user
AI input in Blunt38's databaseNot stored there
Server configuration and feature recordsUntil deleted or no longer needed

Messages, replies, suggestions, and generated ticket transcripts may remain in Discord according to the server's own retention and deletion actions. External providers may apply their own retention periods. Removing Blunt38 from a server stops new processing there but may not automatically erase existing database records; a server owner or affected user can request deletion as described below.

Limited information may be retained longer when reasonably necessary for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legal compliance.

08

Your choices and data requests

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, export, or object to the processing of your personal data. You can also stop using commands, leave a participating server, clear Draw Party browser storage, log out of the dashboard, or ask a server administrator to disable an optional feature.

Request deletion or access

Email lawliet@eclipxse.in with the subject Blunt38 Data Request.

Include your Discord user ID and relevant server ID. Additional verification may be requested to prevent unauthorized deletion.

Verified requests will be handled as soon as reasonably possible and normally within 30 days.

09

Security

Blunt38 uses access controls, signed HTTP-only dashboard cookies, restricted Discord permissions, database security controls, and other reasonable safeguards intended to protect stored data. No internet service can guarantee absolute security. If a material data incident occurs, affected users and Discord will be notified when required.

10

Children

Blunt38 is intended only for people old enough to use Discord under Discord's Terms of Service and the laws of their country. Blunt38 does not knowingly collect personal data from children below the applicable minimum age. Contact the operator if you believe such data has been processed.

11

Policy changes

This policy may be updated when Blunt38's features, providers, or legal obligations change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are published. Continued use after an update means the revised policy applies to later processing.
