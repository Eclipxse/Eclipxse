01

Overview

This policy applies to the Blunt38 Discord bot, its web dashboard, and its Draw Party browser game. It does not cover Discord itself or third-party services you access independently.

Blunt38 processes only the information needed to provide features selected by server administrators or requested by users. Eclipxse does not sell personal data or use it to build advertising profiles.

02

Data processed

The exact data depends on the features enabled in a Discord server.

Discord identifiers

User, server, channel, role, message, moderator, and temporary voice-channel IDs.

Server settings

Configured channels and roles, welcome text, logs, tickets, AI settings, and appearance preferences.

Feature records

Moderation cases, polls and votes, role panels, giveaways, XP and levels, birthday month/day, and temporary voice ownership.

Submitted content

Command inputs, suggestions, ticket messages used for requested transcripts, and messages sent to enabled AI features.

Dashboard account

Your Discord ID, username, avatar URL, and the IDs of servers you are permitted to manage.

Draw Party

Player name, socket ID, room membership, guesses, drawing strokes, scores, and sound preference.

Blunt38 does not request your Discord password. Authentication is handled by Discord OAuth.

03

How data is used

  • Respond to commands and Discord interactions.
  • Run moderation, tickets, roles, polls, giveaways, levels, birthdays, welcome messages, and temporary voice channels.
  • Authenticate server administrators and save dashboard configuration.
  • Provide optional AI replies, music playback, and Draw Party rooms.
  • Prevent abuse, diagnose failures, and protect the service.
  • Meet lawful obligations and enforce applicable platform rules.
04

AI and service providers

When an AI feature is used, relevant message content, instructions, and limited context may be sent to the AI provider configured by the operator: OpenAI, Groq, or OpenRouter. Blunt38 does not store AI prompts in its feature database, although Discord and the selected provider may retain data under their own policies.

DiscordInteractions, OAuth, messages, servers, roles, channels, and voice.
Database hostingSupabase or the configured PostgreSQL host for persistent settings and records.
AI providersOpenAI, Groq, or OpenRouter when an optional AI feature is requested.
Music servicesLavalink and supported sources for searches, links, and playback.
Web infrastructureHosting, CDN, security, diagnostics, and availability.

Eclipxse does not allow these providers to use bot data for Eclipxse advertising.

05

Storage and retention

OAuth stateApproximately 10 minutes.
Dashboard sessionUp to 7 days; stored in a signed, HTTP-only cookie.
Draw Party roomIn memory while active; inactive rooms expire after approximately 3 hours.
Server settings and feature recordsUntil deleted, no longer required, or removal is requested and verified.
Ticket transcriptGenerated on request from recent Discord messages and delivered through Discord.

The Draw Party name and sound preference may be stored in your browser's local storage. Removing Blunt38 from a server does not automatically erase all database records; a server owner may request deletion.

06

Your choices

Server administrators can disable optional features, change configuration, clear browser storage, or remove Blunt38. You may request access, correction, or deletion of data associated with your Discord account or server.

Data request

Email lawliet@eclipxse.in with the subject Blunt38 Data Request. Include your Discord user ID and any relevant server ID.

Additional verification may be required to prevent unauthorized disclosure or deletion. Verified requests are normally handled within 30 days.

07

Safety and age

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect data, including restricted credentials, signed sessions, and access checks. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Blunt38 is intended for people permitted to use Discord under Discord's terms. It is not directed to children below the minimum age required in their country. If you believe a child provided data improperly, contact Eclipxse for deletion.

This policy may change when Blunt38's features or providers change. Material revisions will update the date at the top of this page.

08

Contact

Questions, complaints, and privacy requests can be sent directly to Eclipxse.
